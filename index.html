Secure Staking

PRIDE is a fabulous Cardano stake pool with infrastructure that spans the globe across Asia, Europe, and the Americas for resilient block production and validation.

Value

PRIDE is an Ardana and Hosky stake pool, so you earn 100% of your ADA and are eligible to earn $HOSKY, and eventually $DANA. We'll continue to participate in future ISPOs.

Infrastructure

PRIDE runs 4 high end cloud Linux nodes; 2 relays and 2 block producers across 3 continents. The nodes are monitored closely, backed-up daily, and have SMS paging to resolve any issues. All signing is performed on secure offline cold hosts not on the Internet to protect the keys. 

Commitment

PRIDE is committed to provide value to delegators and the Cardano network by providing great ROI on your ADA, committing to being here for years to come, supporting decentralization, and being involved with the community. We're a mission driven pool that donate to LGBTQIA+ non-profits, and a member of the Cardano Single Pool Alliance. Every year we celebrate #CardanoPride. Thanks for supporting small single pools!
