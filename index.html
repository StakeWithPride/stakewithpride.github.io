SECURE STAKING

PRIDE is a fabulous Cardano stake pool with infrastructure that spans the globe for resilient block production and validation.

PRIDE comprises 5 high end cloud Linux nodes across 3 continents; 2 public relays, 1 hidden relay (to defend against DDoS), and 2 block producers. The nodes are security hardened, monitored closely, backed-up daily, and have oncall SMS paging to resolve any issues. All signing is performed regularly on secure offline cold hosts (not on the Internet) to protect the keys.  

Value

PRIDE is an Ardana and Hosky stake pool, so you earn 100% of your ADA and are eligible to earn $HOSKY, and eventually $DANA. We'll continue to participate in future ISPOs.

Commitment

PRIDE is committed to provide value to delegators and the Cardano network by providing great ROI on your ADA, committing to being here for years to come, supporting decentralization, and being involved with the community. PRIDE is a mission driven pool that donates to LGBTQIA+ non-profits, and is a member of the Cardano Single Pool Alliance. Every year PRIDE celebrates #CardanoPride. Thanks for supporting small single pools!
